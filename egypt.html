How to Get There

Egypt is a land of ancient wonders, from the Pyramids to the Nile. Here’s how to get there:

  • By Air: Cairo International Airport (CAI) is the main gateway, with flights from major cities worldwide. Other airports include Luxor (LXR), Hurghada (HRG), and Sharm El-Sheikh (SSH).
  • By Train: Egypt has a railway network connecting major cities like Cairo, Alexandria, and Luxor.
  • By Cruise: Many travelers take a Nile River cruise between Luxor and Aswan to explore ancient temples.
  • By Bus: Long-distance buses operate between cities, though they can be less comfortable than other options.

Other Places to Visit in Egypt

  • Luxor & The Valley of the Kings – A treasure trove of ancient temples and royal tombs.
  • Abu Simbel – Massive rock temples built by Pharaoh Ramses II.
  • Alexandria – A coastal city known for its historic library, Mediterranean views, and ancient ruins.
  • Siwa Oasis – A remote desert paradise with salt lakes and natural hot springs.
