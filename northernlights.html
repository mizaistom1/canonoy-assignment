Northern Lights, Norway

Why this place is special to me

It's amazing to see this natural phenomena that may look like some other worldy happenings that is consistently occurring on another country specifically, Norway. It looks like the northern lights are coming from a sci-fi movie but it is in fact real. This is the reason why I am so fascinated in this place.

How to Get There

The Northern Lights can be seen in various locations across Norway, with Tromsø being one of the most popular destinations. Here’s how to get there:

  • By Air: The easiest way is to fly into Tromsø Airport (TOS) from Oslo or other major European cities.
  • By Train & Bus: You can take a train from Oslo to Bodø, then continue with a bus or ferry to Tromsø.
  • By Cruise: Many Arctic cruises include Northern Lights tours, offering a unique way to experience them.
  • By Car: Renting a car is an option for exploring the best viewing spots outside the city with less light pollution.

Other Places to Visit in Norway

  • Lofoten Islands – Stunning scenery, fishing villages, and incredible mountain landscapes.
  • Oslo – Norway’s capital, known for the Viking Ship Museum and modern architecture.
  • Bergen – A picturesque coastal city with colorful wooden houses and access to fjords.
  • Svalbard – A remote Arctic archipelago where you can see polar bears and experience the midnight sun.
