Stonehenge, England

Why this place is special to me

I have once seen the stonehenge in England at the news feed of my facebook and it has since then amused me. Questions have repeatedly come through my head, how is the stones structured like that? Who arranged them? I no longer want to look for answers for those questions. I just want to see how beautiful and amazing it looks like in real life and not just in pictures.

How to Get There

Stonehenge is located in Wiltshire, England. The nearest major city is London, about 90 miles away. You can get there by:

  • Train: Take a train from London Waterloo to Salisbury, then a tour bus to Stonehenge.
  • Car: Drive via the M3 and A303 highways.
  • Tour Bus: Many travel agencies offer guided tours from London.

Other Places to Visit in England

  • The Tower of London – A historic fortress with the Crown Jewels.
  • Big Ben & Houses of Parliament – Iconic landmarks in London.
  • Lake District – A beautiful national park with stunning landscapes.
  • Bath – Known for its Roman baths and Georgian architecture.
